Q3 2025 Supply Chain Risk Assessment

Key risks identified during Q3 2025:

1. Semiconductor shortage: Our primary supplier has indicated 8-12 week
   delays for Q1 2026 orders. Mitigation: diversified to 2 additional
   suppliers in Taiwan and Korea.

2. Shipping cost inflation: Container rates rose 23% this quarter.
   Mitigation: renegotiated annual contracts locking in 2025 rates.

3. Single-source dependency: 40% of raw materials still come from one
   region. Recommendation: aggressive dual-sourcing initiative in Q4.

4. Labor shortage at distribution centers: 12% vacancy rate vs 4% in 2024.
   Addressing with automation investments and improved wages.

Overall risk rating: MODERATE (up from LOW in Q2 due to geopolitical factors).
